Team Panchamukhi

We at team panchamukhi make sure that the needy gets helped, at our best. We are service-driven souls, extremely motivated towards serving humanity.

Know the team

VISION

Our vision is to work towards creating a better society which provides basic health, education and empowerment to oppressed citizens. We also strive for better humanity and charity works are our core agenda.

MISSION

We are actively participating in seva programs and our mission is to unite all Hindus by establishing a universal place of worship. We come forward to support economically backward families and one of our goals is to make India hunger free.
